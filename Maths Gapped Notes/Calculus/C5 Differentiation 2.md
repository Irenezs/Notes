# Chain Rule
$$y=f(u(x))\mspace{1em}\text{Then}\mspace{1em}y'=\frac{dy}{du}\times\frac{du}{dx}$$
# Product Rule
$$y=uv\mspace{1em}\text{Then}\mspace{1em}y'=u'v+uv'$$
# Quotient Rule
$$y=\frac{u}{v}\mspace{1em}\text{Then}\mspace{1em}y'=\frac{u'v-uv'}{v^2}$$
